Welcome to Blossom Haven

Step into a world where cherry blossoms never fade and time gently rewinds. At Blossom Haven, our cozy Japanese-inspired café transports you to a nostalgic realm with warm lantern glow, soft tatami mats, and the delicate aroma of matcha and mochi. Savor every moment as past and present intertwine in a haven of serenity.

Menu Highlights

Matcha Serenity

A velvety matcha latte, hand-whisked to perfection, served with a whisper of timeless calm.

Sakura Mochi

Delicate rice cakes filled with sweet red bean paste, wrapped in a salted cherry blossom leaf.

Hojicha Bliss

Roasted green tea with a nutty warmth, paired with a slice of fluffy castella cake.

Yuzu Dream Tea

A refreshing citrus-infused green tea, served chilled with a hint of honeyed nostalgia.

Taiyaki Sunset

Warm fish-shaped pastries filled with creamy custard, dusted with powdered sugar.

Anmitsu Time Capsule

A delicate dessert of agar jelly, red bean paste, and seasonal fruits, drizzled with black sugar syrup.
